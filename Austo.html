Data Dictionary (columns in austo_automobile.csv)

  • Age: Age of the customer
  • Gender: Male/Female
  • Profession: Customers profession
  • Marital_status: Married/Single
  • Education: Highest education (High School, Graduate, Post Graduate)
  • No_of_Dependents: Number of dependents (04)
  • Personal_loan: Whether the customer has a personal loan (Yes/No)
  • House_loan: Whether the customer has a house loan (Yes/No)
  • Partner_working: Whether customers partner is working (Yes/No)
  • Salary: Annual salary of the customer (USD)
  • Partner_salary: Annual salary of partner (USD)
  • Total_salary: Sum of Salary + Partner_salary (USD)
  • Price: Price of purchased car (USD)
  • Make: Car type (Hatchback/Sedan/SUV)

Austo Automobile Buyer Profiling

Problem Statement

Austo (a UK-based auto company) plans to enter the US market. To decide positioning for Hatchback, Sedan, and SUV, we analyze a dataset of 1,581 buyers with demographics, finances, and purchase preferences.

Objectives

  1. Build clear buyer profiles for each car type (Hatchback, Sedan, SUV).
  2. Translate findings into actionable recommendations (pricing, financing, targeting).

def pct_crosstab(index_col, column_col):
    """
    Returns a row-normalized crosstab (% within each row).
    Useful for stacked bar charts showing composition (e.g., Gender mix inside each Make).
    """
    return (pd.crosstab(df[index_col], df[column_col], normalize="index")*100).round(1)

def stacked_bar_from_pct(pct_df, title, xlabel, ylabel="% of buyers"):
    """
    Plots a stacked bar chart from a % crosstab.
    pct_df: index are groups (e.g., Make), columns are categories (e.g., Male/Female).
    """
    ax = pct_df.plot(kind="bar", stacked=True)
    ax.set_title(title); ax.set_xlabel(xlabel); ax.set_ylabel(ylabel)
    plt.legend(title=pct_df.columns.name, bbox_to_anchor=(1.05, 1), loc="upper left")
    plt.tight_layout(); plt.show()

Buyer Personas (derived from analysis)

Hatchback Young Urban Starter

  • Younger age band; lowest median incomes; lower car prices.
  • Messaging: affordability, fuel economy, compact size.
  • Offers: low-APR financing, student/early-career campaigns.

Sedan Mid-Career Family

  • 30s40s with dependents; moderate incomes & prices.
  • Messaging: safety, comfort, reliability.
  • Offers: family bundles, trade-in incentives, insurance partnerships.

SUV Affluent Established

  • Older, highest incomes; highest price points.
  • Messaging: premium features (ADAS, infotainment), space.
  • Offers: extended warranty, premium service packs, loyalty perks.

Recommendations for Austo

  1. Pricing & Trims: maintain a good/better/best ladder (Hatchback < Sedan < SUV).
  2. Financing: personalize APR/down payment by segment (strongest for Hatchback).
  3. Targeting:
    • Hatchback students/early career in urban/campus channels.
    • Sedan family-oriented creatives and safety feature ads.
    • SUV premium placements; dual-income audiences.
  4. CRM: segment by income band, dependents, and loan status; personalize lifecycle journeys.

Limitations & Next Steps

  • Survey sample (1,581 records) may not represent all US buyers.
  • No geographic or lifestyle variables; adding them would refine targeting.
  • Next: validate findings with sales data and run A/B tests on creative/financing offers.
